Road Safety 🚦

What is Road Safety?
Road safety means following rules to stay safe when walking, cycling, or riding in a vehicle. Roads are full of fast-moving cars, buses, and bikes, so it’s important to be careful!

Why is it Dangerous?
🚗 Roads have fast cars that might not see pedestrians.
🚲 Cyclists and pedestrians can get hit if they don’t follow the rules.
🛑 Not paying attention can cause accidents and injuries.

How Can We Stay Safe?
✅ Use the “Stop, Look, Listen, and Think” method before crossing:

STOP at the edge of the sidewalk.
LOOK left and right for cars.
LISTEN for horns or engine sounds.
THINK about whether it's safe to cross.

✅ Always use crosswalks (zebra crossings) and wait for the green signal.
✅ Hold an adult’s hand while crossing the street.
✅ Never run on the road—walk carefully.
✅ Wear a helmet when riding a bicycle.
